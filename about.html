Nice to meet you, I'm Becca!



I'm from suburban Massachusetts where I grew up playing with LEGOs, Microsoft PC & Xbox games, and Nintendo consoles with my family. I made my first video game– a point & click hidden object game programmed in Java– in high school.

By the time I graduated college, I was helping organize the Game Development Club, hosting game jams, and launching a card game on the app stores through MassDiGI's Experience Program. I got my first taste of the industry during a co-op at WB Games Boston and moved halfway across the country to work at SciPlay, where I was able to hone my engineering skills on different teams and features.

The thing I most want to do now is have fun finding out what other kinds of games I can make, and balance my career with a diverse and fulfilling personal life.

Outside of work, I like sitting by the Cedar River listening to music or taking my camera to the woods to go birdwatching. I love libraries, playing games, growing plants and solving puzzles. Some games I'm currently playing are Dwarf Fortress on PC, Pokémon Brilliant Diamond on Switch, and Red Dead Redemption II for PS4.
